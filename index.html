Dr. Yang Wang (汪洋) is an Associate Professor of Physics at the Beijing Institute of Technology. He earned his B.S. and PhD degrees in Materials Science and Engineering from Nanjing University in 2014 and 2019. Dr. Wang joined Beijing Institute of Technology in 2021.

Dr. Wang's research interests include nanophotonics and solar energy conversions.
