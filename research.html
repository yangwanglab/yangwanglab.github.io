Research Areas

Nanophotonics

We are developing metamaterials, metasurfaces, and plasmonic structures for manipulating light, with focuses on optical sensing and spectroscopy, dynamic control of structural color and thermal emission, radiative cooling and thermophotovoltaics.



Solar Energy Conversions

The solar energy that reaches the earth in one hour is enough to supply the energy needs of the earth for a year. However, to make solar energy significant portion of the future energy, the key will be the development of devices and systems that can efficiently and cost-effectively harness and convert solar energy. We are developing solar absorbers, thermal emitters and efficient heat transfer structures for various solar energy conversion systems such as interfical steam generation, solar thermophotovoltaics and solar cells.
